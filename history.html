About the BIP

More than a tournament—an annual tradition built around friendship, competition and the game of golf.

The mission

Keep the tradition alive.

Bring the group together every year, compete hard, travel somewhere memorable and preserve the story for the next generation of BIP players.

The name

Bogey Is Par.

The spirit of BIP is simple: enjoy the round, embrace the competition and never let one bad hole ruin a great trip.

The archive

Every year matters.

This site is designed to store tournament history, results, players, courses, records, photos and the stories behind them.
